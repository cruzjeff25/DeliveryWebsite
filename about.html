ABOUT

This group of problem solvers was frustrated by the process of using call centers to book delivery vehicles, and they set out to develop a digital solution to address this issue. The simple, innovative idea that brought Cargo into existence is that on-demand delivery is possible for everyone with a click. Gaining access to a wide range of well-maintained vehicles is simple thanks to one project.

Start tracking the whole delivery process, use digital payment options, and then access all of your delivery information in one place.

It has become the go-to last-mile delivery solutions for businesses of all sizes. With the help of Cargo , companies in many different industries are able to scale their delivery and outsourcing operations, even if they started out with a brick-and-mortar location.
